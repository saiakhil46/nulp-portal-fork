Raise a Query
What is NULP?
NULP is a digital platform to deliver learning, based on user’s convenience. Built on tenets of peer learning where such users are not only learners, but also creators of content sharing on-ground experience, success stories and insights. NULP's vision is to create an Urban Learning Platform, supplementing traditional capacity building with online learning to enhance skills of urban practitioners in an ever-changing ecosystem.
How can I access the National Urban Learning Platform?
National Urban Learning Platform can be accessed at https://nulp.niua.org/
Who can submit content on the National Urban Learning Platform?
The quadruple helix which comprises the Government, Academia, Industries and the Civil Society Organisations can contribute to NULP by uploading relevant content as per the city's learning needs. Urban experts and practitioners from the Quadruple Helix working on various domains can be content contributors of NULP.
Is there any funding attached for the development of content for the National Urban Learning Platform?
As of now, there is no funding mechanism for NULP partners. However, by end of this year we will be testing out a business model with some of the certification programs. The National Urban Learning Platform showcases content of these organisations at a National scale, which acts for incentives to the content contributors.
What is the difference between Courses and content?
A content is a standalone document which is in a prescribed format such as PDF, Image, Audio-visual file etc.
A course is a structured module prepared out of individual contents and provided as a whole learning package to a learner. On the completion of a course the learner recieves a certificate, however no such certificate is received in the case of content.
How can I get the certificate?
An individual has to enroll in a batch and undertake the course upto its completion.
Upon completion, the learner has to apply for the course certificate (If a certain course requires evaluation, then the learner applies after evaluation).The certificate can be viewed on the learner's profile.
Are these courses free of cost ?
Yes, the courses are free for all urban practitioners as of now. However by the end of this year some of the certification programs will be paid.
Duration of Courses- Can I continue a course that have been left in between without its completion?
The courses are designed in a manner, that it provides bite size learning which can be undertaken in a self paced learning ability pertaining to the individual.
Do I get a personal guidance from the authors of the content whenever required?
Yes you can reach out to us directly thorugh the helpdesk and we will connect you with the partner. Moreover, in many of the courses you will be able to see a contact email ID of the authors in the "About the Course" section
Are these courses available offline / is the content downloadable?
Yes, the content can be accessed offline.
How can I search the topics of my interest?
One can select the topics from the existing category tabs on the website. Also one can type keywords in the search bar and select from the search results.
How can i enroll myself in a course?
1. Signup on the learner's profile on NULP website.
2. To enroll yourself for a course of your interest, check whether the batch is open for enrollment.
3. If the batch is open, you can join the course. In case the batch is closed, one have to wait untill the batch re-opens for enrollment.
Can I enroll myself in multiple courses?
Yes, one can enroll for more than one course at a time.
Is there any course end evaluation?
Course Evaluation depends on the structure of the course.
